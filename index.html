Cassandra Arroyo

is a young woman who dedicated herself to the Spear of Destiny since Brother Gregorio (a monk) explained the truth to her in his monastery. She is part of an ancient bloodline that enables her to change destiny when touching the spear, and is bonded to Asher Gregorio, who keeps her in reality when she is in the void. In a point in her life, Cassie had been hunted by the Hastati, who valued and envied the spear's limitless power. After Sarah Bimington stole the spear, she, as well as Simone and Asher, rescued it and threw it into the sea so destiny could never be controlled again.
