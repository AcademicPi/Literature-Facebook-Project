About Me

My name is Cassandra Arroyo, and I was pulled into the role of a marked one when my father was shot. The

Hastati member kept hunting us until Simone and I escaped to a monk's sanctuary. Brother Gregorio explained

to me that my Type O blood and the unusual mark on my chest defined me as part of an ancient bloodline. I am

one of the few people of this generation who have the ability to use the Spear of Destiny. Once I touch it, if I dare

touch it, I will become bound to it until death, and could harness its power to change the future. The Hastati are

afraid and misguided-- they think that, if they eliminate anyone with the power to alter destiny, the world would be

safe and secure. I am on the run, with my bonded friend Asher and best friend Simone, trying to fend for my own life.

But now it is time to attack, to return fire. I am not a moving target.

Favorite Movie: Ender's Game

This is one of those movies where you have to fast

backward and rewatch several times-- IT'S SO

INTERESTING!!! The way the producers developed

Ender's thoughtful, creative, and audacious

characteristics was entertaining and clever. It also made

me think. During the end, Ender and his team eliminates

the alien race, thinking that they were only in a game.

But when the truth comes out that they had actually

destroyed all extra-terrestrial existence, Ender claims

that the way in which one wins is more important than

the fact that one won. This movie forced me to promise

myself to strategize carefully if I ever use the spear to

alter destiny.
