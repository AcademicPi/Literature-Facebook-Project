About Me

My name is Cassandra Arroyo, and I was pulled into the role of a marked one when my father was shot. The Hastati member kept hunting us until Simone and I escaped to a monk's sanctuary. Brother Gregorio explain to me that my Type O blood and the unusual mark on my chest defined me as part of an ancient bloodline. I one of the few people of this generation who have the ability to use the Spear of Destiny. Once I touch it, if I dare touch it, I will become bound to it until death, and could harness its power to change the future. The Hastati are afraid and misguided-- they think that, if they eliminate anyone with the power to alter destiny, the world would be safe and secure. I am on the run, with my guardian Asher and best friend Simone, trying to fend for my own life. But now it is time to attack, to return fire. I am not a moving target.
